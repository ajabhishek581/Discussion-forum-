Visson and Mission

This is a discussion platform to discuss and learn new ideas.
It achieves and supports students and teachers by facilitating reflection, questioning by self and others,
collaboration and by providing context for engaging in higher order thinking.


Why to Use?

*Provides a good platform to interact with people around the world.
Provides student a better opportunity to learn.
*Motivates students to become better readers and writers.
*Provides teachers and alumni to share their experiences and knowledge with the upcoming talents.
